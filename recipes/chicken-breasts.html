Chicken Breasts

Baking chicken breasts help to reduce the mess when cooking!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Seasonings
  • Chicken Broth

Directions:

Step 1:

Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

Step 2:

Rub chicken breasts with olive oil and sprinkle both sides with salt and Creole seasoning. Place chicken in a broiler pan.

Step 3:

Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes. Flip chicken and cook until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 15 minutes more. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).

Step 4:

Remove chicken to a plate. Pour chicken broth into the pan and scrape any browned bits off the bottom with a flat-edged wooden spatula. Add more broth if needed to dislodge the browned bits, but not too much or it will be watery. Afterward plate it and have fun!
